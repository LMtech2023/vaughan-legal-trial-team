About Vaughan Legal Trial Team

Your Trusted Houston Personal Injury Law Firm

Aggressively fighting for the injured since 2003

After a serious accident or injury, you need a legal team you can count on to fight for the justice you deserve. At Vaughan Legal Trial Team, we represent injured individuals and families in all types of personal injury cases in Houston and the surrounding areas. Since 2003, we have aggressively fought for the rights of those harmed by the negligence of others, offering highly personalized and professional legal services.

Our founding attorney, Jeffery Vaughan, has more than 20 years of trial experience and has recovered more than $100 million in both settlements and jury verdicts. If you or someone you love was injured due to the careless, reckless, or wrongful conduct of another, contact our team today to learn how we can help.

Call us today for a free consultation. You can also reach us online using our secure and confidential contact form.
